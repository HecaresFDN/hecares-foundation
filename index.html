Who We Are

HECARES Foundation is a faith-based youth initiative focused on children, teenagers, and young people. We exist to nurture moral clarity, spiritual growth, and responsible digital awareness, helping young minds understand both life and the future with wisdom.

What We’re Building

We are building a global youth education movement that blends faith, technology, and life skills. Our goal is to prepare young people for the future responsibly — introducing technology, digital systems, and economic awareness without hype or harm.

What We Do

Christian Education

Teaching biblical foundations in relatable, practical ways that shape character and identity.

Digital Literacy

Helping young people understand technology, AI, and digital systems with clarity and responsibility.

Future Readiness

Preparing youths to engage the global digital economy ethically and confidently.

Founder

David King Hecares is a young, passionate African visionary dedicated to building, strategizing, and supporting young people across the globe. His heart is to inspire youths to grow in faith, develop moral strength, and gain early awareness of technology so they can face the future with confidence, clarity, and readiness.
